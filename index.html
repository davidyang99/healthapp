Hematopathology eTutorial





About

Hematopathology eTutorial is a study guide for hematopathology. The course covers benign and neoplastic hematolymphoid diseases in 24 lessons. Each lesson includes an outline-style study note, figures and tables, full-size digital photomicrographs, and audio narrative by an expert hematopathologist. This course can be used as supplemental material to the standard textbooks for pathology residents and fellows, for preparation of clinical pathology and hematology (pathology) board examinations, and as a handbook for pathologists and hematologists/oncologists.
